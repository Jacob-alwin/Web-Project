Who are We

SCJ Entertainments has been founded by Vibhas Joshi, who is a Young Entrepreneur with a Vision to take Indian Cinema and make it a Globally Recognised Sector. SCJ Entertainments is privately held and has fully integrated studio in India to produce Films, Web shows, Short Films and Reality Shows. SCJ Entertainments is a strong production house and a vertically integrated studio in every sense, controlling almost every part of the value chain from production to post production, domestic & international distribution, music & home entertainment, marketing, design, digital, licensing, merchandising, talent management, brand partnerships, music studios, film studios and aggregating partner - all in-house facilities, which make it one of the most coveted entertainment conglomerates in the country.
